# 🗺️ Python Journey — Roadmap

> **My roadmap from Python foundations to professional software development and, eventually, AI Engineering.**

---

## 🌷 My Vision

My goal is to build a strong and complete technical foundation that allows me to become a professional developer and eventually an AI Engineer.

I do not want to rush through Python topics simply to say that I have completed them.

I want to understand what I learn, practice it independently, build with it, revisit my previous work, and improve continuously.

My journey is based on:

**Learn → Practice → Build → Apply → Reflect → Improve → Repeat**

The goal is not to complete a checklist as quickly as possible.

The goal is to become a stronger developer step by step.
